3 Services Overview

Foodys is a technology platform that connects students and staff on university campuses with campus food vendors for the purpose of ordering and receiving meals. Foodys:

  • Provides the app and web platform.
  • Facilitates secure digital payments via Monnify.
  • Assigns employed delivery riders to fulfil delivery orders.
  • Manages the order lifecycle from placement to completion.
ℹ️ Foodys does not prepare food. All food is prepared solely by the vendors listed on the platform. Foodys is not responsible for the quality, safety, or content of food prepared by vendors.

4 Orders & Payments

Placing an order

  • All orders must be placed through the Foodys app.
  • Orders are cashless. No cash on delivery is accepted under any circumstances.
  • Payment is processed at checkout via Monnify (bank transfer). Your order is only confirmed once payment is verified.
  • Orders cannot be modified after submission.

Packs

  • Foodys allows you to split a single order into up to 3 packs — useful for group orders where each person wants their own meal delivered together.
  • A maximum of 3 packs per order is permitted.

Minimum order amount

  • Delivery orders: minimum of ₦500.
  • Pickup orders: minimum of ₦300.
  • Orders below these amounts cannot be processed.

Fees

  • A delivery fee is charged based on the distance zone between the restaurant and your delivery location. The applicable fee is shown at checkout before payment.
  • A service fee of 5% of your food subtotal is added to every order. This fee supports platform operations.
  • Applicable packaging fees for takeaway containers are shown per item and added to your order total.

Delivery windows

  • Foodys operates within defined delivery hours on each campus. Orders cannot be placed outside these hours.
  • An order cutoff time applies before the end of each delivery window. Orders placed after the cutoff will not be accepted.

5 Delivery & Pickup

Delivery

  • Deliveries are carried out by Foodys-employed riders.
  • You are responsible for selecting an accurate delivery location on campus.
  • When your rider arrives, you will receive a push notification. You must collect your order promptly — riders will wait a maximum of 5 minutes at your location.
  • If you cannot be reached after the rider's waiting time, the order may be marked complete without a refund.
  • Estimated delivery times are approximate and may be affected by demand, distance, or other conditions.

Pickup

  • Where a vendor offers pickup, you can collect your order directly from the restaurant at no delivery fee.
  • When your order is ready, you will receive a 4-digit confirmation code via push notification.
  • You must present this code to the vendor to collect your order. The vendor will verify the code to complete the order.
  • Pickup is only available during the vendor's stated pickup hours.

Order confirmation

  • For delivery orders, the rider confirms completion using a code provided by you at the point of handover.
  • If a rider marks an order as arrived and you do not respond within 10 minutes, the order will be automatically completed and your earnings and payout calculations will be finalised accordingly.

6 Cancellations & Refunds

Vendor non-response

  • If a vendor does not accept or begin preparing your order within 10 minutes of payment confirmation, the order will be automatically cancelled and a full refund will be initiated on your behalf.

Vendor rejection

  • A vendor may reject your order if items are out of stock or they are unable to fulfil it. In such cases, you will be notified and a full refund will be processed.

Refund processing

  • All refunds are processed back to your original payment method (bank account used for transfer) via Monnify.
  • Refunds are typically reflected within 24 hours, though processing times may vary by bank.
❌ Orders that have been accepted and are actively being prepared cannot be cancelled. Once a vendor accepts your order, the transaction is final.

No-show & failure to collect

  • If you fail to meet your rider at the agreed location after being notified, or fail to collect a pickup order after it is marked ready, no refund will be issued.

7 Rewards & Points

Foodys operates a loyalty rewards programme to recognise repeat customers.

Earning points

  • Points are earned on completed orders, with bonus points for maintaining an active order streak.
  • Points multipliers apply based on streak length (e.g. 1.2× at 3 days, up to 2× at 7 days).
  • Additional points may be earned through referrals and platform milestones.

Redeeming rewards

  • Points can be redeemed for rewards such as free delivery or a ₦100 discount on your order.
  • Rewards have no cash value and cannot be withdrawn as money.
  • Rewards are only redeemable within the Foodys platform.
  • Some rewards require a minimum number of points to unlock.

Streak restores

  • A broken streak can be restored within 24 hours using a free restore (up to 5 per calendar month) or by spending points.
  • Free restores reset on the first of each month.
⚠️ Foodys reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue the rewards programme at any time. Points have no monetary value and will not be compensated if the programme ends.
