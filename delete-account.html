Request Account Deletion - Foodys

At Foodys, we respect your privacy and allow you to delete your account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Log in to the Foodys app or website using your credentials.
  2. Navigate to your Profile Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account and follow the prompts.
  4. If you need assistance, contact us directly at support@foodysmobile.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, we will permanently remove the following data:

  • Your personal profile information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Your order history and related details
  • Your uploaded photos and media
  • Any linked payment information

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes (e.g., transaction records for tax or compliance reasons) for up to 7 years, but this data will be anonymized and no longer linked to your account.

Additional Information

If you have questions or concerns about your data or this process, please contact us at support@foodysmobile.com.
