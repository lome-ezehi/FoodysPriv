Business Overview

Company Background

Foodys was founded in March 2025 by Ezehi Theddy Oseijoyalome (CEO & Founder) to address the inefficiencies in campus food delivery. The platform officially launched on April 20, 2026, at Veritas University, with plans to expand to additional campuses by Q4 2026.

Value Proposition

For Students: Fast delivery, seamless digital payments, and exclusive campus deals.

For Vendors: Increased sales, digital presence, access to the student market, and a low commission rate of 5%.

For Universities: Enhanced student experience, additional campus revenue opportunities, and job creation within campus.

Product & Platform

Supported Platforms

  • iOS (iOS 13+)
  • Android (Android 8+)

Customer Features

  • Browse vendors and menus with photos, descriptions, and real-time availability
  • Add items to cart with packaging/container customisation options
  • Pack system: Split a single order into up to 3 packs for group orders — each person selects their own meal, one delivery, one payment
  • Real-time order tracking with push notifications at every status change
  • Delivery and pickup fulfilment options
  • Streak and rewards system: earn points on every order, unlock multipliers, redeem for free delivery or discounts
  • Referral code sharing and campus leaderboard
  • Ratings and reviews (eligible only after completing a paid order from the restaurant)
  • Order history

Vendor Features

  • Real-time order notifications with accept/reject actions
  • Order queue management across tabs: New, Ongoing, Pickup, Delivery, Completed
  • Menu management: add, edit, remove items, mark items unavailable
  • Restaurant availability toggle (Open / Closed / Temporarily Paused)
  • Pause feature: pause accepting orders for 15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes during busy periods; auto-resumes when timer ends
  • Prep time tracking with delay alerts
  • Daily and weekly earnings dashboard
  • Analytics: popular items, peak hours, ratings, order metrics
  • Payout dashboard with manual request windows and automatic daily processing

Rider Features

  • Toggle availability (online / offline)
  • Real-time trip assignments with order details and pack counts
  • Delivery sequence display (nearest destination first)
  • Earnings tracker: daily, weekly, monthly
  • Order confirmation via customer-provided code
ℹ️ The platform is fully cashless. No cash is handled by riders at any point. All payments are processed digitally through Monnify at checkout.

Payment Gateway

  • Provider: Monnify (primary)
  • Methods: Bank transfer (via virtual account at checkout)
  • Future: Card payments planned for a later release

Operations & Workflow

Order Flow

1
Customer places order & pays

Cart validated (restaurant open, delivery window active, minimum amount met). Payment processed via Monnify. Order moves to pending.

2
Vendor notified

Push notification and in-app alert sent to vendor. Vendor must respond within 10 minutes. Reminder sent at T+3 min and T+6 min. Auto-cancel and refund fire at T+10 min if no response.

3
Vendor accepts & prepares

Order moves to preparing. Prep time countdown begins. If preparation exceeds the estimated time, vendor and customer are alerted.

4
Vendor marks order ready

Order moves to ready. Vendor payout is triggered. The platform's dispatch engine automatically selects and assigns a rider based on zone and availability.

5
Rider dispatched

Rider receives a push notification and in-app assignment. Orders from the same restaurant are batched where possible (3-minute collection window, max 5 per batch). Rider sees orders sorted nearest-first.

6
Rider collects from restaurant

Rider picks up all bags for their trip. Each order bag is labelled with its order code. If an order has more than 3 packs, it may occupy more than one bag — the rider is shown the total bag count per order to ensure complete collection.

7
Delivery to customer

Customer receives push notification when rider marks arrival. Customer must meet rider within 5 minutes. If unreachable after 10 minutes, the order is auto-completed.

8
Order completed

Rider confirms delivery using the customer's 4-digit confirmation code. Order moves to completed. Rider payout triggered. Customer prompted to rate and review.

⚠️ Rider assignment is fully automated. Vendors do not assign riders. The dispatch engine handles all assignment based on zone, availability, and capacity.

Physical Bag Labelling (Vendor)

When preparing an order for the rider, vendors must label each physical bag with the order code. If an order has more than 3 packs and requires multiple bags, each bag must be labelled:

  • #ABC123 — Bag 1 of 2
  • #ABC123 — Bag 2 of 2

The vendor app displays the required bag count for each order. Riders verify they have collected all bags before leaving the restaurant.

Pickup Orders

For pickup orders (customer collects directly from restaurant):

  • Customer is notified with a 4-digit confirmation code when order is ready.
  • Customer presents code to vendor at collection.
  • Vendor enters the code in the app to complete the order.
  • No rider is involved.

Rider Management & Scheduling

  • Riders work in structured shifts aligned to campus delivery hours.
  • Peak hour incentive: ₦50 bonus per delivery during 12–2pm and 6–8pm.
  • Weekly performance reviews covering acceptance rate, ratings, and delivery time.
  • Mandatory weekly team meeting for feedback and training.
  • Rider support contact available during operating hours for emergencies.

Vendor Onboarding Process

📋 Vendors do not sign a separate contract. By registering on Foodys and listing their restaurant, vendors agree to the Foodys Terms and Conditions, which constitute the full agreement between Foodys and the vendor.
  1. Foodys meets with campus vendor and introduces the platform.
  2. Vendor creates account and registers restaurant in the app.
  3. Menu digitisation: items, photos, prices, categories, and packaging options uploaded.
  4. Training session on vendor dashboard (orders, menu updates, payout requests).
  5. Test orders conducted to verify full flow.
  6. Go-live on platform with promotional support.

Timeline: 2–4 days from registration to activation.

Payout System

The payout system manages vendor earnings through a combination of automated daily processing and manual request windows, ensuring secure and timely fund transfers to vendor bank accounts via Monnify.

Vendor Balance States

StateDescription
Available BalanceFunds from completed orders, ready for payout request
Pending BalanceFunds currently being processed for transfer
Total PaidHistorical record of all completed payouts

Payout Triggers (Automatic)

Vendor balances are updated automatically when orders reach the following statuses:

  • Ready — food is prepared and waiting for rider pickup
  • Pickup Ready — order ready for customer collection (pickup orders)

Manual Request Windows

Morning
12:00 PM
Request window: 12:00 AM – 11:55 AM
Afternoon
3:00 PM
Request window: 12:10 PM – 2:55 PM
Evening
7:00 PM
Request window: 3:10 PM – 6:55 PM
⚠️ 15-minute processing gaps exist between windows (11:55 AM–12:10 PM, 2:55 PM–3:10 PM, 6:55 PM–7:10 PM). Payout requests made during these gaps are not accepted.

Automatic Daily Processing

At 10:30 PM daily, all available vendor balances are automatically swept into pending and processed. Vendors do not need to take any action for this.

Requirements & Constraints

  • Minimum payout: ₦5,000
  • Valid bank account details must be on file before any payout can be processed
  • Foodys is not liable for failed payouts due to incorrect banking details provided by the vendor

Rider Payouts

  • Rider earnings (50% of delivery fee) are credited per completed order.
  • Rider balances are disbursed on a scheduled basis via Monnify to their registered bank account.

Legal & Compliance

Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Full Terms and Conditions are published within the Foodys app and website. Key provisions include:

  • Minimum age of 13 to use the platform
  • Cashless-only payments via Monnify
  • Auto-cancellation and refund if vendor does not respond within 10 minutes
  • 5% commission on food subtotal (vendor) + 5% service fee (customer)
  • Rewards points have no cash value and cannot be withdrawn
  • Foodys disclaims liability for food quality, allergic reactions, or food safety — vendors are solely responsible
  • Data collection limited to operational needs in compliance with applicable Nigerian data protection law

Vendor Agreement

📋 Vendors do not sign a separate contract. By registering a restaurant on Foodys, vendors accept the Terms and Conditions as their binding agreement with Foodys. The T&C constitutes the full vendor agreement, including commission structure, quality standards, and performance expectations.
  • Commission rate: 5% of food subtotal per order
  • Vendor must maintain accurate menus, accept orders within 10 minutes, and prepare food hygienically
  • Repeated unjustified rejections or quality failures may result in suspension

Rider Employment

  • Riders are direct employees of Foodys Enterprise — not independent contractors
  • Rider employment is governed by individual employment agreements and Nigerian labour law
  • Rider compensation: 50% of each delivery fee earned, disbursed on a scheduled basis

Liability

  • Food safety: Vendors are solely responsible for food quality and hygiene. Foodys disclaims liability for food-related illnesses.
  • Delivery: Foodys is not liable for delays caused by traffic, weather, or conditions outside its control.
  • Data protection: Cybersecurity measures are in place. Breach notification protocols are followed in accordance with applicable law.
  • Payment issues: Foodys is not liable for delays or failures caused by Monnify or issuing banks.

Taxes & Regulatory Compliance

  • Foodys operates under Nigerian business law as Foodys Enterprise
  • All applicable taxes are the responsibility of respective parties (vendors for their income, Foodys for platform revenue)
  • Operating permits and campus administration approval obtained per campus
